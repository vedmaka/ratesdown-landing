Sending Your Reports
Send To Anyone

All TPR’s are the same. Why have different forms?

With Notify adjusters use one form for every TPR. No more waiting on the queue to get the email or fax number. No more keeping a list in excel somewhere. No more getting TPR’s with half the fields missing.

Notify let’s adjusters send TPR’s to any insurance company’s preferred method of communication using our dynamic database. And we make sure they can actually process it.
